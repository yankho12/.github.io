CUSTOMS VISION

To be a Modern, Proactive Customs Administration that adapts to the challenges of a changing environment thereby achieving and maintaining excellence in service delivery to all stakeholders.

ASYCUDA offers rich client interfaces.

Full-fledged applications featuring secure e-Documents with rich client interfaces run on any Java-enabled computer connected to the network. They can be launched either from Web browsers such as Netscape Navigator or MS-Internet Explorer, or directly from an icon placed on the desktop.

ASYCUDA runs on Windows XP, Linux, Macintosh, Solaris and others.

The client software is automatically deployed and upgraded using the built-in Java Web Start technology. Most popular desktop operating systems are Windows XP, Linux, Macintosh, Solaris, AIX and others.

Customs Mission

To Effectively and Efficiently Administer the Customs and Excise Act and related laws in order to collect and protect the Customs Revenue, Protect the Safety and Security of Society, and collect accurate trade data while facilitating legitimate trade and travel.
ASYCUDA WORLD

The Malawi Revenue Authority (MRA) seeks to migrate to Automated System for Customs Data (ASYCUDA) World version, a state-of-the-art system and an upgrade from the current ASYCUDA++ version developed by United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD).

The ASYCUDA 2.7 version was upgraded to ASYCUDA ++ version 1.17D which was later upgraded to ASYCUDA ++ version 1.18E in 2007.

MRA therefore seeks to acquire ASYCUDA World version to deliver modern day business requirements by fully embracing recommended best practices as stipulated in the Revised Kyoto Convention and SAFE Framework Standards of the World Customs Organization (WCO)
